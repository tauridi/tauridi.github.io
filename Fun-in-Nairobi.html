Let’s Have Some Fun!

There is something about these days; Saturday, Sunday and the holidays. Something that keeps shouting, let’s go out. Let’s visit a park. Let’s go for a hike. Let’s go for a picnic. Let’s just get out of this house! Interestingly, it’s always ‘let’s’, because human beings are social beings, who just can’t help it but want to be in other people’s company.

A look at some of the activities that could make these noisy days memorable. They are easily accessible, with just a few shillings, you don’t need to save up for months.

Nyama Choma at Carnivore

Where are the Nyama fans? Now this is from my own experience, it’s absolutely amazing, that is, if you enjoy nyamchom. From ostrich meat, kuku, beef, to crocodile meat (it tastes just like chicken, but sweeter). You will just eat until your body can’t take it anymore, it’s just too much! You will have a story to narrate, I promise.

Kenya National Archives

This is one place in Nairobi that must have had so many people pass by. How many people have actually ever gotten inside this building? Come on, let’s not limit it to a landmark! It’s one place that actually narrates the history of our country, you want to know that right? No a boring pile of records, there are so many beautiful stuff in this place.

View the whole of Nairobi from KICC Helipad

Now this is so cheap and you get to enjoy the view of our city from the top, a few feet lower than being in an aircraft. Nairobi is reaaaaally beautiful from up there, just go confirm. And the photos, wow! You want to take photos that appear as though you floating somewhere next to the clouds? Why don’t you try this?

Hike at Ngong Hills

You will enjoy this, actually you will love it, if you go as a team. Hike through the 7 hills at Ngong’…yeah it’s so exhausting, but there’s a fulfillment that comes with it. Though sometimes so foggy (or is it misty?), you will have a beautiful scenery to enjoy the whole time. You could stop right under the huge wind mills and experience a little winter. I must warn you though, next day will need a big body massage.

You could stop right under the huge wind mills and experience a little winter.
Hike/ Ride at Karura

How about some nature? Get a little lost and find your ways through those lanes at Karura forest. When you get in there you actually forget that you are Nairobi, it’s so beautiful. I can’t get the right words to describe. Plus, there are caves… might be cold and dark in there, but it’s lovely. Oh, I almost forgot, there’s also a waterfall that pours so much water during the rainy season. You have the option of walking or riding a bike…enjoy!
